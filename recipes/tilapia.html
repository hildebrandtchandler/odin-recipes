Air Fryer Tilapia

Back to Home

Description

Tilapia cooked in the air fryer, crusted in Parmesan cheese. Serve with rice pilaf or roasted veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 (6 ounce) tilapia fillets, thawed if frozen
  • cooking spray
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley (optional)
  • 4 lemon wedges (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Combine Parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Pat tilapia fillets dry with paper towels.
  4. Spritz both sides of fillets with cooking spray.
  5. Press both sides of the fillets into the Parmesan mixture.
  6. Shake off any excess mixture.
  7. Spray fillet again with cooking spray.
  8. Place fillet in the basket of the air fryer.
  9. Cook fillet until the fish flakes easily with a fork, 6 to 8 minutes.
    You may need to cook the fish in 2 batches, depending on the size of your air fryer.
  10. Sprinkle fillet with parsley and serve with lemon wedges.
