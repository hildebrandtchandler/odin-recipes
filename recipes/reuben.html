Reuben Sandwich

Back to Home

Description

Very delicious and easy to make. Serve with big bowls of steaming vegetable soup and dill pickles on the side.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices rye bread
  • 1/2 cup Thousand Island dressing
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened

Steps

  1. Preheat a large griddle or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Spread one side of bread slices evenly with Thousand Island Dressing.
  3. On four bread slices, layer one slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cup sauerkraut, and a second slice of Swiss cheese.
  4. Top with remaining bread slices, dressing side down.
  5. Butter the top of each sandwich.
  6. Place sandwiches, butter-side down on the preheated griddle.
  7. Butter the top of each sandwich with remaining butter.
  8. Grill until both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side.
  9. Serve Hot.
