Edible Cookie Dough

Back to Home

Description

Egg-free cookie dough to satisfy the craving for chocolate chip cookies without the wait.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Heat-treat your flour so it is safe to use.
    Place flour in a microwave-safe dish and cook for 1 minute and 15 seconds, stirring every 15 seconds. Set aside.
  2. Beat sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a large bowl until creamy.
  3. Beat in vanilla extract and salt.
  4. Add heat-treated flour; mix until a crumbly dough forms.
  5. Stir in milk until dough is just combined.
  6. Fold in milk chocolate chips and mini chocolate chips.
